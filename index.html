Some interesting facts about jaguar

  1. jaguars are the 3rd biggest cat after lion and tigers!
  2. At one time jaguars roamed all the way to the US-Mexico border, but jaguars are now only occasionally sighted in Texas and Arizona
  3. The bite force of a jaguar is about 2,000 pounds per square inch

Some interesting facts about fox

  1. They are members of the Canidae family.
  2. Foxes are omnivorous animals.
  3. The fennec fox is considered to be the smallest species of fox.

Some interesting facts about horse

  1. Horses have around 205 bones in their skeleton
  2. They also have a third eyelid which lies on the inside of the eye and closes diagonally over it for added protection.
  3. Horses produce approximately 10 gallons of saliva a day.

Some interesting facts about penguins

  1. Penguins evolved to fly underwater.
  2. There are about 18 million macaroni penguins in the world. The Macaroni penguins have a lifespan of about 12 years.
  3. While most penguin species can hold their breath for 2 to 3 minutes underwater, but, the large and mighty Emperor penguin can hold its breath for up to 20 minutes under water!
